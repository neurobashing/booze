title: Ward 8

-   2 oz. rye
-   1/2 oz. lemon juice
-   1/2 oz. orange juice
-   2 dashes grenadine

Shake and strain into a cocktail glass. Note that using grapefruit juice
and omitting the OJ, it\'s a
[Blinker](http://localhost/wordpress/index.php/2020/01/19/blinker/).
