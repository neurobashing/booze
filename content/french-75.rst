French 75
#########
:date: 2020-01-19 21:41
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: classic, Gin
:slug: french-75
:status: published

* 1 1/2 oz. gin
* 3/4 oz. lemon juice
* 1/2 oz. simple syrup
* Dry champagne


Shake (sans champagne) with ice, strain into a flute. Top with champagne and garnish with a lemon twist.

Named after `this <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canon_de_75_mod%C3%A8le_1897>`__.
