Moscow Mule
###########
:date: 2020-01-19 19:02
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: ginger beer, vodka
:slug: moscow-mule
:status: published

* 1 1/2 oz. vodka
* 4 oz. ginger beer
* 1/2 oz. lime juice

Combine, stir, do the weird copper mug thing if you have one handy, garnish with lime slice.
