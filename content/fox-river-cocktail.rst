Fox River Cocktail
##################
:date: 2020-01-19 17:01
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: rye, whisky
:slug: fox-river-cocktail
:status: published

* 4 dashes Peach bitters
* 1 lump ice (a perfect cube, obvs.)
* 1 part Creme de Cacao
* 3 parts rye whisky

Shake and strain, or mix right in the glass. Garnish with some lemon peel.
