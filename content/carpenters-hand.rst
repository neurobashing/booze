Carpenter's Hand
################
:date: 2020-01-19 18:00
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: brandy, sherry
:slug: carpenters-hand
:status: published

* 2 oz. brandy
* 1/2 oz. Amontillado sherry
* 1/2 oz. Drambuie (or saffron honey syrup)

Stir in rocks glass, with a big-ass hunk of ice. Garnish with orange peel.
