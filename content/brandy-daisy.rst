Brandy Daisy
############
:date: 2020-01-19 18:52
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: brandy, rum
:slug: brandy-daisy
:status: published

* 2 oz. brandy
* 2 dashes light rum
* 3 dashes orange liqueur (triple sec, etc)
* 4 dashes simple syrup
* 1 tbsp lemon juice

Shake with ice, strain. Optionally add a couple ounces of soda water.
