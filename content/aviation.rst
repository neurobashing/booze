Aviation
########
:date: 2020-01-19 21:32
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: classic, Gin
:slug: aviation
:status: published

* 1 1/2 oz. gin
* 1/2 oz. maraschino
* 1/2 oz. lemon juice
* 1/4 oz. creme de violette

Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Without the creme de violette, it's a Casino.
