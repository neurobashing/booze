Summary: Elk's Own Cocktail

* 1 oz. aquafaba
* 1 part rye
* 1 part port wine
* juice of half a lemon
* teaspoon of powdered sugar

Shake well and strain. Garnish with pineapple.

This is from the 1927 book, Barflies and Cocktails, by Harry Wynn.
