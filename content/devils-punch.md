Title: Devils Punch

* 2 oz. Tequila
* 1 oz. Triple Sec
* 1 oz. Limoncello
* 1 oz. sour mix

Shake and strain into a highball glass. Optionally, add a dash of OJ.

(Yes, you are correct, it's a modified margarita.)
