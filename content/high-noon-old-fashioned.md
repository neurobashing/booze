Title: High Noon Old-Fashioned

* 2 oz. rye
* 1/2 - 3/4 oz. `molasses syrup <http://localhost/wordpress/index.php/2020/01/19/molasses-syrup/>`__
* 1 dash Angostura bitters

Serve in a rocks glass with a proper hunk of ice.
