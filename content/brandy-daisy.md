Title: Brandy Daisy

* 2 oz. brandy
* 2 dashes light rum
* 3 dashes orange liqueur (triple sec, etc)
* 4 dashes simple syrup
* 1 tbsp lemon juice

Shake with ice, strain. Optionally add a couple ounces of soda water.
