Title: Good Luck Charm

* 1 1/2 oz. rye
* 3/4 oz. limoncello
* 2 oz. lemon sour
* a few mint leaves

Muddle the mint in the shaker. Shake and strain into a cocktail glass. Optionally, rim the glass with sugar.

Lemon sour is just simple syrup and some lemon juice.
