Ward 8
######
:date: 2020-01-19 17:29
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: rye
:slug: ward-8
:status: published

* 2 oz. rye
* 1/2 oz. lemon juice
* 1/2 oz. orange juice
* 2 dashes grenadine

Shake and strain into a cocktail glass. Note that using grapefruit juice and omitting the OJ, it's a `Blinker <http://localhost/wordpress/index.php/2020/01/19/blinker/>`__.
