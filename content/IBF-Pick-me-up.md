Summary: IBF Pick-me-up

* 3 dashes Fernet Branca
* 3 dashes Curacao
* 1 glass brandy

Shake well and strain into a medium-sized wine glass. Top with champagne.

(This is a classic recipe from the 1927 book, "Barflies and Cocktails")
