Blood Orange Whisky Sour
########################
:date: 2020-01-19 18:31
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: blood orange, rye
:slug: blood-orange-whisky-sour
:status: published

* 2 oz. bourbon or rye
* 3/4 oz. lemon juice
* 3/4 oz. simple syrup
* 3/4 oz. blood orange juice.

Shake and strain into an old-fashioned glass.
