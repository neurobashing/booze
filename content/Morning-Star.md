Summary: Morning Star


* sweet vermouth
* dry vermouth
* rye
* apple cider
* orange liqueur
* 2-3 dashes Angostura bitters

Shake equal parts, strain into a coupe or martini glass.

You can also use warmed cider to make a more toddy-like drink.
