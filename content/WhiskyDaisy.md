Summary: Whisky Daisy


1 oz Rye
3⁄4 oz fresh lime juice
1⁄2 - 3⁄4 oz agave syrup
1⁄2 oz dry Curaçao (or any orange liqueur) 
chile salt rim, cocktail glass
lime slice garnish

Shake and strain.
