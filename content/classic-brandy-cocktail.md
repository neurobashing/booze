Summary: Old Mr. Boston Classic Brandy Cocktail

* Juice of 1/4 lemon
* 1/4 oz. Curacao
* 1/4 oz. Maraschino
* 1 oz. brandy

Shake well and strain into a cocktail glass. Optionally, frost rim of glass with powdered sugar. Garnish as shown.
