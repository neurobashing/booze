Whisky Sour
###########
:date: 2020-01-19 18:33
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: bourbon, rye
:slug: whisky-sour
:status: published

* 2 oz. bourbon or rye
* 3/4 oz. lemon juice
* 3/4 oz. simple syrup
* egg white or aquafaba

Dry shake (shake without ice) first, then shake with ice. Strain into a rocks glass with ice. Garnish with lemon.
