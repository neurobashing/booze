BQE
###

:slug: bqe
:status: published

* 2 oz. rye
* 1/2 oz. sweet vermouth
* 1/2 oz. dry vermouth
* 1/4 oz. green chartreuse
* 1 dash maraschino

Mix. Probably in a Nick-and-Nora, but whatever's at hand is good. Garnish with a brandied cherry.
