Dark 'n' Stormy
###############
:date: 2020-01-19 21:22
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: rum
:slug: dark-n-stormy
:status: published

* 2 oz. dark rum
* 3 oz. ginger beer

In a highball glass filled with ice, add dark rum and top with ginger beer. Or, add ginger beer and top with dark rum. Whatever. Garnish with lime wedge.
