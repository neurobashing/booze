Metropolitan
############
:date: 2020-01-19 18:53
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: brandy, Vermouth
:slug: metropolitan
:status: published

* 2 oz. brandy
* 1 oz. sweet vermouth
* 1/2 oz. simple syrup
* 2 dashes Angostura bitters

Shake with ice, strain, cocktail glass. Garnish with a cherry.
