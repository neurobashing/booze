Maple Bourbon Smash
###################
:date: 2020-01-19 21:39
:author: admin
:category: Recipes
:tags: bourbon, maple syrup, winter
:slug: maple-bourbon-smash
:status: published

* 2 oz. bourbon
* 1 oz. lemon juice
* 1 oz. maple syrup

Shake and strain into an old-fashioned glass with a single large ice cube, garnish with lemon peel.
