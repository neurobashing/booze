Title: The Savoy Hotel Special Cocktail (No. 1)

* 1 dash Pernod (absinthe)
* 2 dashes Grenadine
* 1 part sweet Vermouth
* 2 parts Dry Gin

Shake well and strain into a cocktail glass. Top with some champagne or prosecco. Garnish with lemon peel.
